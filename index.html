I am a Research Fellow at School of Computing Technologies, RMIT University, under the supervision of Professor Xun Yi. I earned my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science in March 2022. My research pivots on data privacy and security related to machine learning, data mining, cloud computing, and digital health.
